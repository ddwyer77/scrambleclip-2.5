## 1. High-level project overview
Scramble Clip 2.5 — "Shuffle & spice up your vertical clips".
Stack: Python 3, MoviePy, PyQt5 (desktop GUI), FFmpeg.  
Primary goal: let creators batch-generate short (9:16) videos from a folder of source clips, applying smart scene selection, optional AI effects, text captions, audio beds, and (new) transparent overlay videos.  Current features: GUI for paths & options; smart clip picker; batch rendering with FFmpeg post-processing; packaging scripts for macOS DMG / ZIP.
